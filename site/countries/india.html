Is Prediction Market Trading Legal in India?

Prediction markets exist in a legal gray area in India. Key points:

  • Polymarket: Available and accessible in India. Not explicitly blocked. India is Polymarket's second-largest market by traffic.
  • Kalshi: Announced India access as part of its global expansion in 2025. Available but with limited payment options.
  • Domestic platforms: India has its own prediction/opinion trading platforms like Probo and TradeX, which operate under the "game of skill" exemption.
Legal Note: India has no specific legislation governing international prediction markets. Trading on Polymarket/Kalshi is not explicitly prohibited, but also not explicitly regulated. Crypto regulations in India are evolving. Consult a local legal advisor for personal guidance.

Tax Implications for Indian Traders

Prediction market earnings may be subject to Indian tax laws:

  • Crypto gains tax: India charges 30% flat tax on crypto profits (Section 115BBH). Since Polymarket uses USDC, profits may fall under this.
  • TDS: 1% TDS on crypto transactions above INR 10,000/year
  • Income tax: Prediction market profits could also be classified as "income from other sources"
  • Recommendation: Consult a CA (Chartered Accountant) for your specific situation
Important: Keep records of all trades and deposits for tax purposes. Both Polymarket and Kalshi provide transaction history that you can download.

FAQ — Prediction Markets in India

Is Polymarket legal in India?

Polymarket is accessible and not blocked in India. There is no specific Indian law prohibiting the use of international prediction markets. India is Polymarket's second-largest market by traffic. However, the regulatory landscape is evolving.

How do I deposit INR on Polymarket?

Polymarket accepts USDC (crypto). Buy USDC on an Indian exchange like WazirX or CoinDCX using UPI/bank transfer, then transfer USDC to your Polymarket wallet via Polygon network. Alternatively, use Polymarket's built-in card on-ramp with a Visa/Mastercard.

Which prediction market is best for Indian users?

Polymarket is the best choice for Indian traders due to zero fees, wide market selection, and easy crypto deposits. India also has domestic platforms like Probo for INR-based trading.

Do I need to pay taxes on prediction market profits in India?

Likely yes. Crypto profits in India are taxed at 30% flat rate. Prediction market earnings through Polymarket (which uses USDC) would fall under crypto taxation. Consult a Chartered Accountant for advice specific to your situation.
