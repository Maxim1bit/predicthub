Step 2: Send USDC to Polymarket

  1. On your exchange, go to Withdraw USDC

Short answer: Yes, it's accessible and not blocked. India has no specific law prohibiting international prediction markets. The platform is accessible without VPN. India is Polymarket's second-largest traffic source globally.

My advice: Use Polymarket freely but keep clean tax records. The regulatory landscape in India is evolving — declaring your crypto income properly is the best protection.

FAQ — Polymarket India

Can I deposit directly with UPI on Polymarket?

Not directly. You need to buy USDC first on an Indian exchange (WazirX, CoinDCX, Binance) using UPI, then transfer USDC to Polymarket via Polygon. Takes 8-12 minutes total.

How much tax do I pay on Polymarket profits?

30% flat tax on crypto gains under Section 115BBH. Plus 1% TDS on transactions above ₹10,000. No deductions allowed. Consult your CA for your specific situation.

Is my money safe on Polymarket?

Polymarket runs on Polygon blockchain — your funds are in a smart contract, not held by a company. All trades are verifiable on-chain. The platform processes $2.5B+ weekly. That said, prediction markets carry trading risk — you can lose money on bad predictions.
