FAQ

Can I deposit Naira directly on Polymarket?

Not directly. Buy USDC through Binance P2P (bank transfer), Luno, or Quidax. Then transfer USDC to Polymarket via Polygon network. Takes 10-20 minutes.

Is my money safe in USDC?

USDC is a stablecoin pegged 1:1 to the US dollar. It's backed by reserves at regulated US banks. Actually, keeping funds in USDC can protect you from Naira depreciation — it's effectively saving in dollars.

How do I withdraw to my Nigerian bank account?

Withdraw USDC from Polymarket → send to Binance → sell USDC for NGN via P2P → receive bank transfer. Reverse of the deposit process.
